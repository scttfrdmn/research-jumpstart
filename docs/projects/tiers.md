# Understanding Project Tiers

Research Jumpstart organizes projects into three tiers based on complexity, time commitment, and learning objectives. This tiered approach helps you choose the right starting point for your research journey.

## Overview

| Tier | Name | Duration | Projects | Best For |
|------|------|----------|----------|----------|
| **1** | Flagship | 4-5 days | 3 | Production research workflows |
| **2** | Complete | 2-3 days | 11 | Full domain analysis |
| **3** | Starter | 2-4 hours | 7 | Learning fundamentals |

## Choosing Your Tier

### Start with Tier 3 if you...
- ‚úÖ Are new to the domain or cloud computing
- ‚úÖ Want to complete something in a few hours
- ‚úÖ Are teaching or learning fundamentals
- ‚úÖ Need to explore before committing resources
- ‚úÖ Don't have an AWS account (Studio Lab)

### Move to Tier 2 if you...
- ‚úÖ Have domain knowledge and research questions
- ‚úÖ Need complete analysis pipelines
- ‚úÖ Are working with real datasets
- ‚úÖ Want reusable research workflows
- ‚úÖ Are ready for 2-3 days of focused work

### Jump to Tier 1 if you...
- ‚úÖ Have production research needs
- ‚úÖ Need AI-enhanced insights
- ‚úÖ Require distributed computing
- ‚úÖ Are working with large-scale data
- ‚úÖ Want to demonstrate cloud capabilities
- ‚úÖ Have team collaboration requirements
